COIR PITH

Forms


Loose Form

Coir pith can only be used in its loose form. Due to its density, the cost of transportation tend to be very high.


5 Kg Bricks

Due to it's light density, coir pith is compressed Hydraulically to predce the cost of transportation. These are the most products for household and open farms.


650 Gram Bricks

Similar to the 5Kg Block, the 650 Gms brick makes it easier to handle and use.


Grow Bags

A very popular form is to put compressed boards of coir pith inside plastic bags with cuts for plants in nurseries and green houses. All bags come with a 2 year UV treatment certification.
We have recently introduce the option of having certified biodegradabe plastic wrapping making easier to for farmers to plant from the nurseries.


Open Top Grow Bags

The smart home gardening solution!
Open top grow bags provide the ideal solution for having your own home garden- it can hold indiviual plants or a group of collective plants, helping to distinguish one from the other. It doesn't demand attention, not does it require maintenance. The best part of it all is that is reusable.
