COIR PITH

What is it?

An Excellent soil conditioner! Along with a very high water holding capacity, it acts as a buffer in high temperatures and malnutrtion. It also keeps the soil loose and well aerated, thereby enabling vigorous root and plant growth.

We cater to a wide variety of plants and farming practices. We offer differnt types of coir pith in a variety of forms.



Types Forms
The coir pith can be bought raw or processed depending on the plants needs. Most flowering plants thrive in the raw form while vegetables do well in the washed form and fruits in the chemically stabilised forms. The coir pith is a very light and porous material and is therefore often transported after being highly compressed. The compression can take many forms depending on how the customer intends to use it. Some of the common ideas have been listed on our website. We cater to all needs.
  • Unwashed (high EC)
  • Washed (Low EC)
  • Buffered (chemically treated and stabilised)
  • Chips
  • Partially Decomposed
  • Loose Form
  • 5Kg Bricks
  • 650 Gms Blocks
